RUSSIA: REBEL ASSAULT  Chechen rebels mounted an unusual daytime attack, underlining their growing boldess by ambushing a Russian military convoy in the secessionist republic's capital, Grozny, killing 3 Russians and wounding 17, officials said. The rebels set off a mine under one of the trucks in a Russian convoy, then opened fire on the soldiers with automatic weapons and grenade launchers.
